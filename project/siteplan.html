Site Plan

This is the site plan for the WDD 231 course.

Site Name

Restaurant and Apartment Guide in Accra

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about high-quality restaurants and rental apartments in Accra.

Target Audience

Tourists and residents looking for a good dining experience or a suitable apartment in Accra.

Scenario

Where can I get the best restaurant in town?

where can I book a suitable apartment?

Color Scheme

The color scheme for this site will be a combination of #974444 and white, representing professionalism and cleanliness.

Typography

The typography for this site will be a clean, modern font that is easy to read and provides good contrast against the background.

font-family: Google Sans - To be used for headings

font-family: Times New Roman - To be used for body text

Wireframe

The wireframe for this site will include a header with the site name and navigation, a main content area with the site's primary information, and a footer with copyright information.
